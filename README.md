# Stop the ICE Facility in Surprise, AZ

Community action to oppose the proposed 1,500-bed ICE detention facility in Surprise, Arizona.

## üö® The Situation

On January 23, 2026, DHS purchased a 418,400 sq ft warehouse at 13290 W Sweetwater Avenue for $70 million. **The City of Surprise was not consulted.** This facility is planned for 1,500 detention beds and sits on the same street as Legacy Traditional School (1,868 students).
